Stephanie Houghton is an Associate Professor of Instruction in the Department of Economics at the University of Texas at Austin.

Her research areas include empirical industrial organization and applied microeconomics, and she has published in the American Economic Review and RAND. Stephanie is passionate about teaching and mentoring students in her role as the Director of the Master's Program in Economics . Originally from Texas, she received her B.A. in Economics and B.S. in Business Administration from Trinity University in San Antonio. She completed her Ph.D. in Economics at Duke University before returning to her home state, where she and her husband now raise two kids and a rambunctious doggo.
